Simple Salmon Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Salmon (fillet)
  • Onion (1 stalks)
  • Garlic (1 cloves)
  • Olive oil (1 tbs per fillet)
  • Salt (1 tsp each)
  • Pepper (1 tsp each)
  • Thyme (1 tsp each)

Procedure

  1. First clean the salmon well
  2. After place the salmon in tin foil wrapp that is in the shap of baskits
  3. Add the salt, pepper and thyme to a bowl then spread across the salmon
  4. Add Olive oil and spread it over each fillet, you can estimate it
  5. be sure to crush the garlic and peel it before cutting
  6. Cut both the onions and garlic into around 1inch pieces
  7. Add the garlic and onions into the baskets with the fish
  8. Seal off each basket
  9. Place into an oven set to 420 degrees fahrenheit for around 20 minutes
